✅ Licensing Checklist
Your Path to a Life Insurance License
Follow these steps in order. Most people complete the process in 2–6 weeks.
  • 1
    Complete Pre-Licensing EducationFinish your state's required hours of life & health insurance education. (Usually 20–40 hours depending on your state.) Use the Pre-Licensing Course button on the previous screen.
  • 2
    Schedule & Pass Your State ExamRegister for your state's life & health licensing exam through Pearson VUE or Prometric. Study your pre-licensing materials and pass with a 70% or higher.
  • 3
    Complete Fingerprinting & Background CheckMost states require fingerprinting as part of your application. Schedule this at an approved location in your state.
  • 4
    Apply for Your State LicenseSubmit your application through your state's Department of Insurance (NIPR.com works for most states) and pay the licensing fee.
  • 5
    Complete Contracting with NAAOnce licensed, submit your contracting paperwork with National Agents Alliance to get appointed with carriers.
  • 6
    Book Your Onboarding Class & Get StartedGet plugged into your onboarding training, set up backoffice access, and start your first sales week.

💡 Why Get Licensed?

Picture this — you wake up when you decide, work from wherever you want, and your income is limited only by your effort and ambition. No ceiling. No clock to punch. Just you, a phone, and the ability to change lives.

When a family loses someone they love, one of the first calls they make is to an insurance agent. You become the person who shows up when it matters most — the one who hands them a check that keeps the lights on, the mortgage paid, and the kids in school. You become the reason they made it through the hardest year of their life.

Most American families are one emergency away from financial disaster — and most of them don't know it yet. You do. That's not just a career opportunity. That's a calling.

Your license unlocks:

  • Unlimited earning potential — top agents earn $10K–$30K+ per month
  • True time freedom — set your own schedule, work from anywhere
  • A career with purpose — protect real families at their most vulnerable moments
  • A proven system — leads, training, and a team behind you from day one
  • A path to agency ownership — build a team and unlock higher contracts

The only thing standing between you and that life is a licensing exam. And we will walk you through every single step to get there.

The question isn't whether you can do this. The question is — how soon do you want to start?
